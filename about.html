About Me

Hello! I'm Codealuxz, a passionate photographer and developer capturing moments through my lens.

Photography is more than just a hobby for me - it's a way to tell stories, freeze emotions, and share my perspective of the world. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new places with my camera.

I specialize in landscape, street, and portrait photography, always looking for unique angles and lighting to create compelling images.
